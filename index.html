About Me

Hi I'm Tommy. I enjoy creating mechanics and gameplay that makes others smile or feel challenged. My goal as a game designer/programmer is to give back the joy I found in games growing up. As a young lad, my favorite game by far was Minecraft, and many others could say the same thing. If I ever had the chance to create something like Minecraft, which had the power to influence generations of kids, I would say I have completed my biggest career goal. Currently I enjoy playing League of Legends a lot, but my favorite game genre definitely has to be rogue-likes.
