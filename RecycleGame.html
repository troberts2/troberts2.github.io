Project Details

R & R & R is a game centered on climate change. In our group, we were given the prompt climate change with no other requirements. Originally I wanted to make a polar bear platformer where you escaped melting iceburgs, but our group decided to do a more edutainment style of game. In this game all about recycling, you are essentially a recycling plant worker. You have a conveyer belt in front of you and the options to cut, wash, or melt the items that come towards you. You then have to make a decision on whether or not to actually recycle the object if of course it is recyclable.
